Only work once. Do not click them more than once.

Refreshes all other abilities once.

PLAYER

Instructions

hp = Hull Points. When your hp reaches zero, you lose.

sp = Shield Points. When your sp reaches zero. Repair your 'shield' and restore to 50 sp.

shield = When functioning, prevents damage to hp.

weapon = Allows your ship to fire automatically at your enemy when functioning. Click to repair.

defense = Your ship's defensive system. Will intercept enemy fire. Higher block chance when shield is functioning. 100% hit chance by enemy when defense is down.

damage = Amount of damage your ship can inflict on enemy.

OBJECTIVE

Repair your modules by clicking on the 'Click to repair' word when it appears.

Keep your ship alive and destroy your enemy!
